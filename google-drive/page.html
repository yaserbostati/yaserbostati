Google Drive - Better File Upload

UX Research, Flow Redesign, Prototyping

2025

Why file upload?

Uploading is the first action most users take when using Google Drive. Despite its importance, the upload experience suffers from lack of feedback and inefficiencies. This creates stress, especially in time-sensitive or high-stakes situations

Discovery & Ideation

  1. User reviews

125+ user reviews from the App Store, Google Play, Reddit, and forums that were demonstrating difficulty with upload

  1. Heuristic evaluation

Before anything else I had to evaluate and test the current upload process for Google Drive using Nielsens 10 Usability Heuristics

  1. Competitors analysis & inspiration

Analyzing competitors gave me better insight about the features that Google Drive lack and others have which helped decision makeing and advanced progress.

  1. User flows

I jotted down initial structure of how the user flow is going to be for the new upload process.

  1. Wireframing Solutions

I designed low-fidelity wireframes to outline the structure and test ideas before designing the final product


Upload Process

  1. Destination Picker

Before upload starts, a lightweight modal appears.

Options: the modal includes Last-used folder, default folder, or Browse folders
Includes ability to create a new folder on the fly

  1. Upload Manager Panel

A persistent, collapsible side panel that appears when uploads begin.

The panel shows all queued files, real-time progress, speed, and ETA
Allows per-file actions: Pause, Cancel, Retry, View in Drive
Statuses: Uploading, Complete, Failed, Paused

  1. Failure Recovery & Messaging

when a file fails to upload the panel shows clear reason and Offer Retry, Delete, or Download logs

User Testing

Methodology

Participants: 6 users (3 professionals, 2 students, 1 designer)

  • Platforms tested: 4 desktop (Mac & PC), 2 mobile (Android)

  • Prototype fidelity: Mid to high, built in Figma with functional clickthroughs

  • Session format: Remote moderated via Zoom and Lookback; 2530 minutes each

Findings:

Upload Manager Panel

  • Users liked the visibility of progress but expected a clearer indicator of the icon buttons do after the file is complete

Destination Picker

  • 3 users missed the option to create a new folder until they hovered around.

  • Users wondered if they can indvidualy choose to replace or keep duplicate files

  • Some were unsure whether recent folders included shared folders.
