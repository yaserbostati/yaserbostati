Sunstop Sunscreen

This is a concept for a sunscreen company. My mission was to establish a brand identity that exudes playfulness and seamlessly embodies the essence of the product which is a sunscreen cream. With a non-specific target audience, the primary focus was to create a captivating design that instantly ignites a desire to experience the product firsthand.

In crafting the brand identity, I deliberately selected primary beach colors to create a contrasting effect with the black and white logo. This color scheme was chosen to evoke a sense of summer, aligning with the product’s peak usage period. To convey a friendly and approachable vibe, I opted for a round script typeface, which not only adds a touch of smoothness but also reflects the creamy texture of the product. The logo mark, symbolizing a drop of the cream, harmoniously complements the chosen typeface, resulting in a cohesive and visually appealing brand identity.
